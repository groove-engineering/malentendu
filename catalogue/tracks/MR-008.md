# MR-008 — Casket Carousel

- **Fusion:** Noise rock × Electroclash (via PFFR + Captain Ahab)
- **Backend:** Suno
- **Crossing:** `poc/crossings/noise-rock-x-electroclash.yaml`
- **Audio:** [MR-008-casket-carousel.mp3](MR-008-casket-carousel.mp3)

## Prompt

```
ravesploitation, glitchy electro-horror, 140 BPM jumpstyle kick pattern,
manic vocoder yelps over lurching distorted synth bass, lo-fi drum machine
pushed into clipping, queasy detuned melody like a broken music box on a
children's show that shouldn't exist, bursts of guitar feedback cutting
through the beat like a saw, the groove never stops even when the sounds
are eating themselves, gabber energy with no-wave dissonance, absurdist
confrontational, simultaneously hilarious and terrifying, Residents-style
uncanny electronics meet Speedo-clad dancefloor chaos, exquisite-corpse
song structure where sections don't resolve they just mutate
```

## Exclude

```
industrial metal, clean synth-punk, polished EDM, serious dark techno,
Nine Inch Nails, pretty noise, shoegaze, winking ironic pastiche, retro
synth-wave nostalgia, anything that takes itself seriously enough to be
cool, anything too cool to be funny
```
