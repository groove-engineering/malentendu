# MR-009 — Jaleyka dans la Serre

- **Fusion:** Musique traditionnelle de l'Atlas × Electroclash × Synthwave noise (Death Grips)
- **Backend:** Suno
- **Crossing:** multi-atom (musique-atlas-jajouka, electroclash, synthwave-noise-hip-hop, field-recording, koto, vielle-à-roue, jaleyka)
- **Audio:** *(pending)*

## Prompt

```
psychedelic spatial soundscape, field recording textures woven throughout,
polyrhythmic alternation between ternary (6/8) and quaternary (4/4
four-on-the-floor) time signatures, traditional Atlas mountain music
percussion (tbel drums, hand claps, ghaita-like drones) interlocking
with koto arpeggios — channeling the trance repetition of the Master
Musicians of Jajouka, hurdy-gurdy (vielle à roue) as lead melodic voice
droning and grinding over the polyrhythm, abstract crescendo building
intensely danceable energy, party-restarting momentum,

KICK STRUCTURE: no kick drum on the entire track EXCEPT two windows —
first appearance: 36 beats then disappears, second appearance: returns
2 minutes later and stays from minute 4 to minute 6 only,

VOCALS: female voice — horror/slasher-film witch with a Slavic accent,
incantatory, between whispered threats and possessed screaming,
lyrics in franglais (French-English mix, switching mid-sentence),
then grizzly bear howls drenched in cathedral reverb replacing the voice,

intermittent jaleyka (Russian traditional shepherd's flute) theme
recurring throughout — groovy, melodic, folklorique but rhythmically
locked to the Atlas-trance grid,

TWO INTERLUDES (~50 seconds each): synthwave noise hip-hop à la Death
Grips, heavy swing feel, abrasive distorted 808s, shouted vocals,
glitched synths, noisy and aggressive but swinging hard,

FINAL SECTION: pagan sacrificial ceremony incantations in franglais
processed through electroclash production — vocodered, monotone,
over crude TR-808 kick and minimal throbbing bass, ritualistic and
danceable, the ceremony becomes the groove, anti-climate-change
invocations reframed as occult liturgy,

overall: a track that restarts a dead dancefloor, the abstraction
never kills the body, the weirdness serves the swing, every element
exists to make someone move who had stopped moving
```

## Exclude

```
ambient drift without pulse, new age meditation, world music smoothie
that flattens the sources into one exotic texture, clean EDM drops,
generic festival builds, anything that resolves the tension into comfort,
straight grid quantization killing the swing, ironic detachment that
refuses to commit to the groove, psytrance arpeggios, prog rock
self-indulgence, anything too long between moments of physical impact,
samba, bossa nova, Brazilian percussion, Latin rhythms, belly dance
clichés, orientalist exotica, clean pretty witch vocals (must be
terrifying not seductive)
```

## Notes de registre

- 🎼 **Musicologique** : polyrythmie 6/8 ↔ 4/4, musique traditionnelle de l'Atlas (tbel, ghaita, transe répétitive — réf. Master Musicians of Jajouka), koto (gamme pentatonique miyako-bushi), vielle à roue (bourdon + mélodie), jaleyka (échelle diatonique, ornements traditionnels russes), electroclash (120–135 BPM, TR-808, vocoder monotone)
- 👂 **Ressenti** : montée en puissance abstraite et intensément dansante — relancer une fête — climat de cérémonie occulte terrifiante et extatique
- ✊ **Politique** : incantation païenne sacrificielle — le discours écologiste recodé en liturgie occulte, la parole militante n'est plus sermon mais rituel collectif, le dancefloor comme espace cérémoniel (forme et non contenu, méta-guardrail §7)
